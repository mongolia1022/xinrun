Aout our company

Foshan Xinrun Electronics Technology Co.,Ltd is professional manufacturer of designing and producing special lighting electronic ballast and UV germicidal lamps, products including UV electronic ballast、UV germicidal lamps,metal halide (MH) electronic ballast、high pressure sodium (HPS) electronic ballast. UV electronic ballast widely used in sewage treatment, air purify, waste gas treatment and all kinds of places that need sterilization and decontamination; Metal halide (MH) electronic ballast not only widely used in commercial lighting and road lighting but also special lightings such as pet lighting, aquarium lighting and plant growth lighting ect; High pressure sodium (HPS) electronic ballast apply to plant growth lighting and road lighting ect.We insist on ” Innovation is the soul, quality is fundamental, honesty is brand”as our enterprise mission.

We are committed to be a trusted and respected supplier of lighting power supply product and lamps. According to ISO9000 quality control system to design and manufacture electronic ballast and lamps, we focus on production process control, quality inspection and control costs, and devoted to producing high quality, competitive price and environment friendly lighting products, now some products reach the leading level at home and abroad. OEM and ODM service are provided.
